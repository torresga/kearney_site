Dear Friends,

"Thank you for all your support in 2004, 2006, and again in 2008. Each election was better than the one before. You displayed lawn signs, contributed money, volunteered your time, worked on election day, publicly endorsed my campaign, and voted for me. You had other choices, but decided that a thoughtful, family based, justice oriented, law abiding approach to state government is best.

Primary election day is Tuesday May 18th. The polls open at 7 AM.

Right now, I am the only announced candidate from any party for the 172nd District seat of the Pennsylvania General Assembly. This will change, but it is indicative of my commitment to the election and to the important issues affecting the district.

With your help, I am going to win the Democratic Party's Primary Election on Tuesday May 18th, and then the General Election in November.

I invite you to join my campaign. I invite you to run for committee person in your division, and marry our campaigns together. Come help me govern, because government does matter."

-Tim Kearney
